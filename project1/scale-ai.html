About us
Why SCALE.AI ?
Over the years, software development has gotten more and more complex, deploying an application requires you to interact with lots of complex systems that may slow down the development process. We understand that this proccess can be made much easy and accesible.
AI has experiment a massive growth over the years, as it can be used in a wide variety of applications, we aim to provide and easy way to implement these systems in your already existing or new software.
If you have ever used a mainstream service provider for cloud based solutions, you may have noticed how much of a hassle is to get personal treatment, requiring you to have engineers that really understand them. On of our main goals is to provide you with fast and efficient support so you can focus on your technology.
How?
You might be wondering how do we achieve to make deployment easier, well, the answer is in our name, we use Machine Learning models that automatically handle everything according to your needs.
